Tortillas

Description

There isn’t much that is better than taco night at home…. unless you also make your own tortillas! I discovered how ridiculously easy these are to make when I had a midnight taco craving and lacked the get-up-and-go to make a TB run (and that means a lot… I do love a cheesy gordita crunch!).

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour, all-purpose
  • 1 tablespoon butter, room temperature
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon taco seasoning (optional, but delicious!)
  • ¾ cup water

Steps:

  1. Combine all dry ingredients with the butter into mixer with dough hook.
  2. Mix until roughly combined.
  3. Add water and mix until smooth dough forms, about 2 minutes.
  4. Remove and divide into 10-12 portions.
  5. Roll out into circles on lightly floured board with floured rolling pin. Aim for dime thickness.
  6. Heat dry skillet over medium/slightly above medium heat.
  7. Cook dough circles in dry pan until golden bubbles form. Flip and cook slightly to color other side.
  8. Stack with paper towels between to prevent soggy tortillas.
